Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GA Sticks

Does GA Stick harm snakes or other animals?

No, GA Stick does not harm snakes or any other animals. It uses vibration technology to create an uncomfortable environment that snakes naturally avoid, without causing any physical harm.

How many units do I need for my space?

For a small garden (up to 50 sq.m), 1-2 units are sufficient. For larger areas like farms, we recommend placing sticks every 6-8 meters along the perimeter. Contact our support team for personalized recommendations.

How long does the battery last?

The 2000mAh battery lasts approximately 2 weeks on continuous use with standard vibration mode. On eco mode, it can last up to 4 weeks. Charging takes about 4-5 hours with a standard USB charger.

Is it safe for pets and children?

Yes, absolutely! GA Sticks are completely chemical-free and use only vibration. The vibrations are imperceptible to humans and most pets, but effectively deter snakes.

What is your warranty policy?

All GA Sticks come with a 1-year manufacturer's warranty against defects. We also offer 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the product.
